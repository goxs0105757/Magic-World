## 🎮 How to Use Magic-World

Magic-World is made for simple and fun interaction. Here’s how to get the most out of it.

### Exploring Worlds

- Use your mouse or finger (on a touchscreen) to click or tap areas in the video.
- Drag the view around to see different parts of the world.
- Watch as the video changes and new scenes appear based on your actions.

### Creating Your Own Worlds

- Click the “Create” button in the main menu.
- Choose from a selection of starting environments.
- Add objects or characters by selecting them from the sidebar.
- Use simple sliders to adjust speed, lighting, or animation effects.

### Saving and Sharing

- When you are happy with a video world, click “Save.”
- Give your session a name and save it locally.
- To share, export the video clip as an MP4 file.
- Share the MP4 on social media or send it directly to friends.

## ⚙️ Settings and Preferences

Magic-World offers basic settings so you can tailor the experience:

- **Sound Volume:** Adjust the volume or mute sounds entirely.
- **Interaction Speed:** Slow down or speed up how quickly the world responds to your input.
- **Language:** Select your preferred language from the options menu.
- **Reset to Defaults:** Returns all settings to the original configuration.

Access settings anytime by clicking the gear icon in the upper right corner of the app.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting and Tips

- **The app won’t open:** Restart your computer and try again. Make sure your system meets the requirements.
- **Video is choppy or slow:** Lower the video quality in settings. Close other apps that may use a lot of memory.
- **Saving doesn’t work:** Check that you have enough free space on your disk and permission to write files.
- **App crashes:** Try reinstalling from the download page. If it persists, contact support with a description of the issue.
